# HealthShield Plus - Coverage Details

## Policy Number: HSP-2025-TN-001

## 1. Inpatient Hospitalization

### 1.1 Room and Board
- General Ward: Fully covered
- Semi-Private Room: Covered up to Rs. 4,000 per day (Silver), Rs. 6,000 per day (Gold), Rs. 10,000 per day (Platinum), No limit (Diamond)
- ICU/ICCU: Covered up to 2x the room rent limit
- Minimum 24-hour hospitalization required

### 1.2 Covered Inpatient Services
- Surgeon, anesthetist, and specialist fees
- Operation theatre charges
- Medicines, drugs, and consumables during hospitalization
- Diagnostic tests and imaging (X-ray, MRI, CT scan, ultrasound)
- Blood transfusion and oxygen charges
- Prosthetic implants and medical devices (up to 50% of sum insured)

### 1.3 Pre-Hospitalization Expenses
- Covered for 30 days prior to admission
- Includes consultations, diagnostic tests, and medications related to the hospitalization

### 1.4 Post-Hospitalization Expenses
- Covered for 60 days after discharge
- Includes follow-up consultations, diagnostic tests, medications, and physiotherapy

## 2. Outpatient (OPD) Coverage

### 2.1 OPD Benefits (Gold tier and above)
- Doctor consultations: Covered up to Rs. 500 per visit, maximum 20 visits per year
- Diagnostic tests: Covered up to Rs. 5,000 per year
- Pharmacy: Covered up to Rs. 3,000 per year for prescribed medications

### 2.2 Preventive Health Check-up
- Annual health check-up covered up to Rs. 2,000 for all tiers
- Available after completion of 1 continuous year of coverage

## 3. Day Care Procedures

The following day care procedures (requiring less than 24 hours of hospitalization) are covered:
- Cataract surgery
- Dialysis
- Chemotherapy and radiotherapy
- Tonsillectomy
- Lithotripsy (kidney stone treatment)
- Arthroscopy
- Angioplasty
- Dental surgery requiring hospitalization
- Endoscopic procedures
- And 140+ additional listed day care procedures

## 4. Maternity Benefits (Platinum and Diamond tiers)

### 4.1 Coverage
- Normal delivery: Covered up to Rs. 35,000
- Cesarean section: Covered up to Rs. 60,000
- Pre-natal and post-natal expenses: Covered up to Rs. 5,000
- Newborn baby coverage from birth for 90 days

### 4.2 Waiting Period
- Maternity benefits activate after 24 months of continuous coverage
- Limited to 2 deliveries during the policy lifetime

## 5. Critical Illness Cover

### 5.1 Covered Critical Illnesses
The policy covers the following critical illnesses with a lump sum payout equal to 100% of the sum insured:
1. Cancer (malignant tumors)
2. Heart attack (first myocardial infarction)
3. Stroke resulting in permanent disability
4. Kidney failure requiring dialysis
5. Major organ transplant (heart, liver, lung, kidney, pancreas)
6. Coronary artery bypass surgery
7. Aorta surgery
8. Multiple sclerosis

### 5.2 Conditions
- 90-day survival period after diagnosis required for payout
- Waiting period of 90 days from policy start for critical illness claims

## 6. Ambulance Charges

- Emergency ambulance: Covered up to Rs. 2,500 per hospitalization
- Air ambulance (Diamond tier): Covered up to Rs. 2,50,000 per incident

## 7. Exclusions

### 7.1 Permanent Exclusions (Never Covered)
- Cosmetic or aesthetic surgery (unless required for reconstruction after an accident)
- Weight management treatments, obesity surgery (unless life-threatening)
- Self-inflicted injuries or attempted suicide
- Injuries from participation in hazardous activities (skydiving, bungee jumping, etc.)
- Treatment related to alcohol or drug abuse
- Dental treatment (unless hospitalization is required)
- Spectacles, contact lenses, and hearing aids
- Infertility treatments and assisted reproduction (IVF, IUI)
- War, terrorism, or nuclear contamination related injuries
- Experimental or unproven treatments

### 7.2 Waiting Period Exclusions
- Pre-existing conditions: 24-month waiting period
- Specific illnesses (hernia, cataract, joint replacement, kidney stones, gallbladder stones): 24-month waiting period
- Maternity: 24-month waiting period
- Initial waiting period: 30 days from policy start (except accidents)

## 8. Co-payment and Deductibles

### 8.1 Co-payment
- Silver tier: 10% co-payment on all claims
- Gold tier: No co-payment
- Platinum tier: No co-payment
- Diamond tier: No co-payment
- Senior citizens (aged 61+): Additional 10% co-payment on all tiers

### 8.2 Deductibles
- No deductible on Silver and Gold tiers
- Voluntary deductible option available on Platinum and Diamond:
  - Rs. 25,000 deductible: 10% premium discount
  - Rs. 50,000 deductible: 15% premium discount

## 9. Network Hospitals

### 9.1 Cashless Treatment
- Available at 5,000+ network hospitals across India
- Pre-authorization required at least 48 hours before planned hospitalization
- Emergency cases: Notification within 24 hours of admission
- Cashless facility subject to policy terms and network hospital agreement

### 9.2 Reimbursement
- Available for treatment at non-network hospitals
- Claims must be submitted within 15 days of discharge
- Reimbursement processed within 30 days of complete documentation submission
