# HealthShield Plus - Claims Procedures

## Policy Number: HSP-2025-TN-001

## 1. Types of Claims

### 1.1 Cashless Claims
Cashless claims can be availed at any of the 5,000+ network hospitals. Under cashless treatment, the insurer directly settles the bill with the hospital, and the policyholder only pays any co-payment or non-covered charges.

### 1.2 Reimbursement Claims
If the policyholder receives treatment at a non-network hospital or if cashless authorization is not obtained, they may submit a reimbursement claim after paying the hospital bill.

## 2. Cashless Claim Process

### 2.1 Planned Hospitalization
1. Contact the TPA (Third Party Administrator) helpline at 1800-XXX-XXXX at least 48 hours before admission
2. Submit the pre-authorization form at the hospital's insurance desk
3. Required documents for pre-authorization:
   - Health card or policy number
   - Photo ID of the insured
   - Doctor's prescription recommending hospitalization
   - Estimated treatment cost from the hospital
4. TPA processes the request within 24 hours
5. Approval, partial approval, or rejection communicated to hospital and policyholder
6. Upon discharge, settle any co-payment or non-covered charges directly

### 2.2 Emergency Hospitalization
1. Get admitted to the nearest network hospital
2. Notify the TPA helpline within 24 hours of admission
3. Submit the pre-authorization form through the hospital's insurance desk
4. TPA processes the emergency request on priority (within 4 hours)
5. Follow the standard discharge process

## 3. Reimbursement Claim Process

### 3.1 Steps to File a Reimbursement Claim
1. Notify the insurer within 24 hours of hospitalization (for emergencies) or before admission (for planned)
2. Collect all original documents from the hospital upon discharge
3. Fill out the claim form (available at www.tnimpact-insurance.com/claims or from the TPA)
4. Submit the completed claim form along with all required documents within 15 days of discharge

### 3.2 Required Documents for Reimbursement
- Duly filled and signed claim form
- Original hospital discharge summary
- Original hospital bills and receipts (itemized)
- Original pharmacy bills with prescriptions
- Original diagnostic and lab test reports with bills
- Doctor's consultation notes and prescriptions
- FIR copy (in case of accident)
- MLC (Medico Legal Certificate) if applicable
- Pre-hospitalization bills (within 30 days prior)
- Post-hospitalization bills (within 60 days after discharge)
- Copy of health card and photo ID
- Bank account details (cancelled cheque or bank statement)
- NEFT/RTGS form for electronic transfer

### 3.3 Claim Processing Timeline
| Stage                           | Timeline              |
|---------------------------------|-----------------------|
| Document submission deadline    | 15 days from discharge|
| Acknowledgement of claim        | Within 7 days         |
| Query raised (if documents incomplete) | Within 15 days  |
| Response to query deadline      | 30 days from query    |
| Claim settlement (if complete)  | Within 30 days        |
| Claim settlement (if queried)   | Within 15 days of receiving complete documents |

## 4. Claim Settlement

### 4.1 Settlement Modes
- **Cashless:** Direct settlement with the network hospital
- **Reimbursement:** Electronic transfer (NEFT/RTGS) to the policyholder's registered bank account
- **Cheque:** Only if electronic transfer is not feasible

### 4.2 Claim Amount Calculation
The claim amount is calculated as:
```
Claim Amount = Eligible Expenses - Co-payment - Deductible - Non-covered Items
```

Where:
- Eligible expenses are those covered under the policy terms
- Co-payment percentage depends on the tier (10% for Silver, 0% for Gold/Platinum/Diamond)
- Deductible applies only if voluntarily opted
- Non-covered items include items listed under exclusions

## 5. Claim Rejection Reasons

A claim may be rejected due to:
1. Non-disclosure or misrepresentation of material facts at the time of enrollment
2. Treatment for excluded conditions or during the waiting period
3. Treatment at a non-recognized hospital
4. Incomplete or fraudulent documentation
5. Claim filed after the submission deadline (15 days from discharge)
6. Policy lapsed due to non-payment of premium
7. Treatment not medically necessary as per the insurer's medical team assessment
8. Expenses exceeding sub-limits without prior approval

## 6. Appeals Process

### 6.1 Internal Appeal
1. If a claim is partially or fully rejected, the policyholder receives a written explanation
2. The policyholder may file an appeal within 30 days of receiving the rejection letter
3. Submit the appeal with additional supporting documents to: appeals@tnimpact-insurance.com
4. The appeals committee reviews the case within 15 business days
5. Decision communicated in writing within 5 business days of the review

### 6.2 External Grievance Redressal
If unsatisfied with the internal appeal decision:
1. **Insurance Ombudsman:** File a complaint with the Insurance Ombudsman within 1 year of the rejection. The Ombudsman's decision is binding on the insurer.
2. **IRDAI Grievance Cell:** Register a complaint on IGMS (Integrated Grievance Management System) at igms.irda.gov.in
3. **Consumer Forum:** File a case with the District/State/National Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission

## 7. Fraud Prevention

### 7.1 Anti-Fraud Measures
- All claims are subject to verification and investigation
- The insurer reserves the right to conduct medical examination of the insured
- Hospital visits and audits may be conducted by the TPA
- AI-based fraud detection system screens all claims

### 7.2 Consequences of Fraud
- Immediate policy cancellation without refund
- Rejection of all pending claims
- Recovery of any fraudulent claim amounts paid
- Legal proceedings as per applicable laws
- Reporting to the Insurance Information Bureau (IIB)
