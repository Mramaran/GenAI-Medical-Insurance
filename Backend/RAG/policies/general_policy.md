# HealthShield Plus - General Health Insurance Policy

## Policy Number: HSP-2025-TN-001

## 1. Policy Overview

HealthShield Plus is a comprehensive health insurance plan offered by TN IMPACT Insurance Corporation. This policy provides financial coverage for medical expenses incurred by the insured individual and their eligible dependents.

**Policy Type:** Individual and Family Health Insurance
**Underwriter:** TN IMPACT Insurance Corporation
**Regulatory Authority:** Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India (IRDAI)
**Policy Year:** April 1, 2025 - March 31, 2026

## 2. Eligibility

### 2.1 Who Can Enroll
- Individuals aged 18 to 65 years at the time of enrollment
- Spouse of the primary insured (aged 18 to 65 years)
- Dependent children aged 91 days to 25 years
- Dependent parents aged up to 70 years (with additional premium)

### 2.2 Pre-Enrollment Requirements
- Completed application form with accurate personal and medical history
- Medical examination required for applicants aged 45 years and above
- Disclosure of any pre-existing medical conditions
- Identity proof (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport)
- Address proof (utility bill, bank statement)

## 3. Enrollment and Effective Dates

### 3.1 Enrollment Period
- Open enrollment: January 1 to March 31 each year
- Special enrollment within 30 days of qualifying life events (marriage, birth of child, loss of other coverage)

### 3.2 Effective Date
- Coverage begins on the first day of the month following enrollment and payment of the first premium
- Waiting period of 30 days from the effective date for non-emergency treatments
- Pre-existing conditions waiting period: 24 months from the effective date

## 4. Premium Structure

### 4.1 Annual Premium Rates
| Coverage Tier    | Sum Insured    | Annual Premium |
|------------------|----------------|----------------|
| Silver           | Rs. 3,00,000   | Rs. 8,500      |
| Gold             | Rs. 5,00,000   | Rs. 14,000     |
| Platinum         | Rs. 10,00,000  | Rs. 24,000     |
| Diamond          | Rs. 25,00,000  | Rs. 48,000     |

### 4.2 Premium Adjustments
- 5% loading for each year of age above 45
- 10% family discount when enrolling 3 or more members
- No-claim bonus: 10% cumulative increase in sum insured per claim-free year (max 50%)
- Premium payment modes: Annual, Semi-Annual (2% surcharge), Quarterly (5% surcharge)

### 4.3 Premium Payment
- Grace period: 30 days from the due date
- Policy lapses if premium is not paid within the grace period
- Reinstatement possible within 90 days of lapse with payment of outstanding premiums

## 5. Policy Renewal

### 5.1 Renewal Terms
- Policy is renewable annually on the anniversary date
- Lifelong renewability guaranteed regardless of claims history
- Renewal premium may be revised based on age band and claim experience
- Renewal notice sent 30 days before the expiry date

### 5.2 Portability
- Policyholders may port to another insurer while retaining continuity benefits
- Portability request must be made at least 45 days before renewal date
- Waiting period credits for pre-existing conditions are transferred

## 6. General Conditions

### 6.1 Disclosure
- The policyholder must disclose all material facts accurately
- Non-disclosure or misrepresentation may lead to policy cancellation or claim rejection

### 6.2 Territory
- Coverage is valid across all hospitals in India listed in the network
- International coverage available only under the Diamond tier for emergency hospitalization

### 6.3 Cancellation
- Policyholder may cancel within 15 days of issuance (free-look period) for a full refund minus proportionate risk charges
- After the free-look period, cancellation refund is prorated based on unused coverage period
